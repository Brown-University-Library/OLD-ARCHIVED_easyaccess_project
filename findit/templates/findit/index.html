Article Examples

  • Held in print only. (should show holdings location)

Book Examples
  • Requestable -- no alternate versions. (should redirect to easyBorrow landing page; should show 'Request' button)
  • Requestable -- ebook also available.
    (should redirect to easyBorrow landing page; should show 'Request' button, and show ebook link)
  • Not requestable -- local copy available.
    (should redirect to easyBorrow landing page; should _not_ show 'Request' button, should show minimal info about available copies, and show the new-josiah bib-link)
  • Not requestable -- local copy available; and Hay and ebook and 'other editions' available.
    (should redirect to easyBorrow landing page; _not_ show 'Request' button, and show minimal info about available print, ebook, and Hay copies, and minimal info about other-editions available, and show the new-josiah bib-link)
