Important Notice

Checking-out of physical materials from Brown University Library, interlibrary loan, and BorrowDirect have been suspended until further notice. In the meantime, patrons are asked to keep any items now in their possession -- including those obtained through Borrow Direct, easyBorrow, and ILLiad -- until the libraries reopen. All fines and late fees will be waived.

While you may submit new requests, they will be held for future processing. We will reassess all Library services on an ongoing basis.

Check the Library's COVID-19 announcements page frequently for updates about Library services.
